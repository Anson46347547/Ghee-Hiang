GHEE HIANG WITH
HEART & SOUL

Ghee Hiang's roots in the 1856 boom of the tin mining and rubber industries in Malaya led to the establishment of its renowned traditional pastries. The brand's commitment to quality persisted with the introduction of Ghee Hiang Baby Brand Pure Sesame Oil in the 1900s. Today, the brand is a trusted name for households and commercial use, embodying Malaysia's rich heritage and traditions while continually improving its products and techniques.
