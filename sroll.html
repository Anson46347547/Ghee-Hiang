History of Ghee Hiang

Founded in 1856
Leading Heritage Brand in Penang
Products include:
~Sesame Oil
~Pastry
~Beverage

Mass Migration of Chinese Coolies
Ghee Hiang was founded by the Teng's Family
Beginning of Ghee Hiang traditional pastries

Since 1926

Ghee Hiang was sold to the Ooi and Ch'ng Family

Since 1930

Ghee Hiang Sesame Oil was introduced

Since 1962

Ghee Hiang Sesame Oil Factory was established
Ghee Hiang Coffee & Beverages was introduced
